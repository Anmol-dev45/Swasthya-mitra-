Critical rules

rule 1
No everyone decides everything
Everyone contributes, but one domain owner makes the final call. No endless debates.
rule 2
Time-box every discussion
Hard cap at 15 to 20 minutes. If stuck, move forward and adjust in the next iteration.
rule 3
Default to action
When stuck, build a quick version. Test it. Improve it. Never block on certainty.
rule 4
Respect domain lines
Do not override UX on UX. Do not override dev on tech. Authority is distributed, not chaotic.
